The Game Rules

1. The game is round based.

2. Player rolls two dices and moves fields based on value rolled.

3. Players have money that they can buy properties or pay rent with.

4. Player can buy an unowned property that his pawn stands on.

5. If player stands on another players' property, he has to pay rent.

6. Rent is calculated on the base rent price and amount of houses owned.

7. Every time player passes the start field, he gets $200.

8. If player stands on Go to Jail field, he goes to jail for one turn.

9. If player owns all properties of one color, he can buy houses on those properties.

10. If, when paying rent, players' balance goes below $0, he loses the game.

11. After 50 rounds, the player with the highest balance wins.
